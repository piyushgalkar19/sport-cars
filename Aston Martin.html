Aston Martin DB9 GT

The Aston Martin DB9 GT was the ultimate, final iteration of the iconic DB9 (2015-2016), boasting a more powerful 540bhp V12 engine, sharper looks with black splitter/diffuser, upgraded interior tech (AMI II, Apple CarPlay), carbon ceramic brakes, and adaptive suspension, delivering the "very best of DB9" experience before the DB11 arrival, blending timeless design with enhanced performance.

.

Key Features:

  • Engine:

    5.9L Naturally Aspirated V12

  • Power:

    540 bhp

  • Torque:

    ~457 lb-ft (620 Nm)

  • 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph):

    4.5 seconds

  • Top Speed:

    183 mph (295 km/h)

  • Transmission:

    6-speed Touchtronic II Automatic

  • Chassis:

    VH Platform (Aluminium/Magnesium Alloy)

  • Brakes:

    Carbon Ceramic Discs (Front 398mm, Rear 360mm)
