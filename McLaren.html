McLaren GT

The McLaren GT is a luxurious, mid-engined V8 grand tourer blending supercar performance (620hp, 0-100km/h in 3.2s) with everyday usability, featuring a unique MonoCell II-T carbon fiber chassis, adaptive suspension, and surprising luggage space (over 20 cubic feet) for long trips, succeeding the GT model with the newer GTS. Key info includes its 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8, 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, RWD, butterfly doors, and focus on comfort via Proactive Damping Control.

Key Features & Information

Performance & Engine

  • Engine:

    4.0L M840TE Twin-Turbo V8

  • Power:

    620 PS (612 bhp) / 630 Nm torque

  • 0-100 km/h:

    3.2 seconds

  • Top Speed:

    326 km/h (204 mph)

  • Transmission:

    7-speed Dual-Clutch (SSG)

  • Drivetrain:

    Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)

  • Chassis:

    Carbon Fibre MonoCell II-T

  • Suspension:

    Adaptive Double Wishbone with Proactive Damping Control

  • Seating:

    2-seater
