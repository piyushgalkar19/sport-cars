BMW M5

The BMW M5 is a legendary high-performance luxury sedan (and Touring wagon) blending track capability with everyday usability, now featuring a powerful M Hybrid V8 powertrain with a twin-turbo V8 and electric motor, delivering over 700 horsepower, blistering acceleration (0-100 km/h in ~3.5s), intelligent M xDrive all-wheel drive, and luxurious M-specific interiors with advanced tech, making it a top contender in the executive sports car segment.

    Key Features & Specs (Current Generation)

  • Powertrain:

    M Hybrid System: High-performance M TwinPower Turbo V8 engine + electric motor.

  • Power:

    Combined system output of ~727 hp (535 kW) and 1,000 Nm torque.

  • Acceleration:

    0-100 km/h in approximately 3.5 seconds.

  • Drivetrain:

    M xDrive all-wheel-drive (with RWD option).

  • Transmission:

    8-speed M Steptronic automatic.

  • Body Styles:

    Sedan and Touring (wagon).

  • Hybrid Tech:

    Plug-in hybrid with usable battery capacity (e.g., 18.6kWh), electric-only range (e.g., 67-69km), and fast charging.
