Lamborghini Sian

The Lamborghini Sián (FKP 37 & Roadster) is Lamborghini's first hybrid supercar, using a V12 engine with a revolutionary supercapacitor for instant power boost, delivering over 800 horsepower, speeds exceeding 350 km/h, and 0-100 km/h in under 2.8 seconds, featuring extreme futuristic design with aerodynamic wings, Y-shaped lights, smart materials like shape memory alloys, and extensive carbon fiber for an unparalleled power-to-weight ratio.

    Key Features & Technology

  • Hybridization & Supercapacitor:

    World's first use of a supercapacitor in a production car, storing 10x the power of a Li-ion battery but being 3x lighter, providing instant torque fill and charging/discharging efficiency.

  • Engine:

    Upgraded 6.5L V12 with titanium valves, producing 785 CV (577 kW), boosted by a 34 CV electric motor for a combined 819 CV (602 kW).

  • Performance:

    0-100 km/h in under 2.8s; top speed over 350 km/h (220 mph).

  • Design:

    Futuristic, angular design with active aerodynamics, carbon fiber splitters, hexagonal accents, and signature Y-shaped headlights and interior elements.

  • Materials:

    Extensive use of carbon fiber and advanced materials, including passive shape memory alloys for automatically opening cooling flaps, reducing weight.

  • Name Meaning:

    "Sián" means "flash of lightning" in Bolognese dialect; "FKP 37" honors Ferdinand Karl Piëch (born 1937).
