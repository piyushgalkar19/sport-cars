Ford Mustang GT

The Ford Mustang GT is a performance icon featuring a powerful 5.0L V8 engine, delivering thrilling acceleration and sound, with modern tech like customizable digital dashes, Track Apps for performance metrics, and available 10-speed automatic or manual transmissions with paddle shifters, offering a blend of raw power, classic muscle, and advanced driving features in stylish Fastback or Convertible bodies.

.

Key Features:

  • Engine:

    5.0L Ti-VCT V8.

  • Horsepower (Approx):

    Around 480-486 hp (varies by year/exhaust).

  • Torque (Approx):

    Around 418 lb-ft (with active exhaust).

  • Transmission:

    Available 6-speed Manual or 10-speed Automatic with Paddle Shifters.
