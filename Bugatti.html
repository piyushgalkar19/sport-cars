Bugatti Chiron

The Bugatti Chiron is a legendary hypercar known for its extreme performance, blending luxury with raw power from its quad-turbo 8.0L W16 engine, delivering around 1,500+ horsepower, all-wheel drive, and top speeds exceeding 260 mph (420 km/h), with special editions like the Super Sport breaking 300 mph (490 km/h) using advanced aerodynamics, carbon fiber, and exquisite hand-crafted interiors, epitomizing ultimate automotive engineering and exclusivity

.

Key Features:

  • Engine:

    8.0L Quad-Turbocharged W16.

  • Power:

    ~1,103 kW / 1,500 PS (Standard); 1,176 kW / 1,600 PS (Super Sport).

  • Torque:

    1600 Nm (1180 lb-ft).

  • Top Speed:

    Over 261 mph (420 km/h); Super Sport 300+ reached 304.773 mph (490.484 km/h).

  • Acceleration:

    0-100 km/h in 2.4s; 0-300 km/h in 12.1s (Super Sport).

  • Drivetrain:

    All-Wheel Drive, 7-speed DSG gearbox.
